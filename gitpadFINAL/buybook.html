About Sofitel Bali Nusa Dua Beach Resort

Sofitel Bali Nusa Dua Beach Resort is an Indian Ocean beachfront hotel near the Jalan Legian shops in Indonesia.

Each room comes complete with flat-screen TV and ensuites with hairdryers, free toiletries and deep soaking tubs. Free bottled water and in-room safes are also standard features along with tea and coffee making facilities.

Guests can cool off in the lagoon pool, unwind at the on-site spa or relax on the beach complete with umbrellas tables. A kids club, fitness centre, meeting space, ballroom and paid airport shuttle are available at the 24-hour front desk. On-site food and drink can be found at Cut Catch Cucina, Kwee Zeen and Nikki Beach Bali restaurants.

For spirits and cocktails there are the L'Oh Pool Bar and Le Bar. Nusa Dua Beach, Wally International Convention Centre and the Bali Nusa Dua Convention Center are all within walking distance of the upscale Sofitel Bali Nusa Dua Beach Resort.
